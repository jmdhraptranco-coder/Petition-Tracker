System Capabilities
What Portal Nigaa Offers
📊

Real-Time Dashboard

Comprehensive overview with status indicators, priority levels, and aging analysis across all APTRANSCO circles and divisions.

🔄

Workflow Automation

Automated routing, escalation timers, and status updates ensure petitions move through investigation stages without manual intervention.

🏢

Multi-Office Tracking

Track petitions across Corporate Office, all CVO offices, and field divisions with hierarchical access controls.

📈

Analytics & MIS Reports

Generate MIS reports, trend analyses, and compliance summaries for board presentations and review meetings.

🔔

SLA & Alert System

Automated reminders for pending actions, SLA deadline breaches, and escalation triggers keep investigations on track.

🔐

Secure & Auditable

Role-based access control with complete audit trails. Every action is logged for full transparency and accountability.

Ready to Access Portal Nigaa?

Staff members with authorised credentials can log in to manage, track, and resolve vigilance petitions across APTRANSCO.
